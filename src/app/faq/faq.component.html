CAMUP Frequently Asked Questions

1. What states does CAMUP currently cover?

CAMUP’s database includes missing and unidentified persons cases from Louisiana, Arkansas, Texas, and Mississippi.

2. What should I do if I believe I’ve found a match?

Please submit any potential information via the NAMUS website or contact your local law enforcement agency.

3. Will you expand to other states?

Yes. CAMUP will eventually include all 50 states.

5. How can I report an error or request data corrections?

If you notice any inaccuracies, please use our email us at support@camup.com with the case number and details.
