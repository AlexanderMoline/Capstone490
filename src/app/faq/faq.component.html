CAMUP Frequently Asked Questions

1. What states does CAMUP currently cover?

CAMUP's database includes missing and unidentified persons cases from the following states: Louisiana, Arkansas, Texas and Mississippi.

2. If I think I have a relevant lead or information what should I do?

If you believe that you have information that may be useful to authorities, please submit a request on the NAMUS website .

3. Will you expand to other states?

Yes, CAMUP will eventually include the rest of the states.
